Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to information by:

  • Creating a unified digital repository of books, manuscripts, and reference material.
  • Offering multilingual content to cater to India's diverse population.
  • Integrating modern technology like AI search, Braille tools, and audio libraries.
  • Collaborating with national and regional libraries to preserve cultural heritage.

What We Offer

1. Vast Collection: Over 500,000 digital resources including eBooks, academic papers, scanned archives, and journals from top institutions across India.

2. Regional Libraries: Featuring detailed information and collections from premier libraries like Rampur Raza Library, National Library of India, and Anna Centenary Library.

3. Accessibility: Tools for visually impaired users such as text-to-speech, audiobooks, and Braille content. All materials are optimized for mobile and low-bandwidth access.

4. Research Assistance: Integrated citation tools, search filters, and metadata access for academic research.

5. Language Support: Resources available in English, Hindi, Tamil, Urdu, and other major Indian languages.
